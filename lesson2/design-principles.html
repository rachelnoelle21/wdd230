White Space & Clean Design

Nike

nike.com


Nike's website is a great example of how to correctly use white space. Throughout their website there is a comfortable amount of empty space. This space allows the readers eye to rest.

Visual Hierarchy

Adidas

adidas.com


Adida's website is a great example of how to correctly use visual hierarchy. This placement of images and text allows the readers eye to find what is most important. My eye goes directly to the button.

Rule of Thirds

Puma

Puma.com


Puma's website is a great example of how to correctly use the Rule of Thirds. Throughout their website we see implementation of photography, text, and white space is separated into thirds.
