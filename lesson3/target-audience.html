Target Audience

The Chamber of Commerce Target Audience

The target audience of this website consists of men and women ages 40-70. These people enjoy traveling and being outdoors. They like to walk, run, or bike the Greenbelt. These people enjoy nature, but also love to grab a bite to eat downtown. Whether these people are from Idaho Falls or not, they all have something in common; they all love to look at the Idaho Falls temple.

Personas

Dwight

Dwight is a man in his 60's. He owns property in Idaho Falls, but is originally a farmer in Driggs. He likes to visit Idaho falls and walk around the downtown area. His favorite place to eat there is The SnakeBite Restaurant. Afterwards, he likes to walk around the river and enjoy the breezy wind that always lingers in Idaho.

Angela

Angela is a woman in her 40's. She lives in Idaho Falls near the river. Her family goes to the river often on walks around the Greenbelt. Her favorite thing to do is spend time with her family. She loves to go to City Bagels and Bakery after and she always gets an Italian Garden bagel.

Scenarios

First Scenario: Where is the best place to eat in downtown Idaho Falls?
The best place to eat is a new restaurant called Smokin Fins in Idaho Falls. It is located near the river. It serves everything from fish and chips to 52-day aged Ribeye.

Second Scenario: What stores are located near downtown Idaho Falls?
The shops located downtown are unique and not at all common. The best shops include Poppy and Pout Boutique, The Handmade Idaho Shop, and Lemon Meringue. All of these shops sell cute items from clothing to trinkets.
